Benefits of WASP

In this recent era, 90% of the world’s goods rely on maritime transportation thus it contributes to 3% of the world’s greenhouse gas emissions. Lately, the International Maritime Organization has set a target emission goals of 70% by 2050. To achieve this goal, some researchers has shown some evidence of WASP as a strong applications to achieve this goal.

Fuel Savings

Understanding Fuel Savings & WASP technology

By implementing Wind assisted ship technology, one of the big anticipations is reduced fuel consumption. As a means, many researchers have developed multiple different methods that can be implemented on the ship deck. Below, we explore its history and different approaches to fuel savings.

  • Rotor sails
  • Hard sails
  • Sunction wings
  • Kites
  • Soft sails
  • Wind turbines

Quantifying the savings

According to the International Windship Association website, there are different levels of fuel savings percentages we can expect depending on the different types of motors the ship applies. Wind-Assisted Motor Vessels offer 10-30% fuel savings and purely wind-assisted vessels can contribute to the 100% of fuel savings.

Emissions

Environmental impact
Another big element of implementing WASP technology is reducing greenhouse gas emissions. However, the performance of wind-assisted ship technology heavily relies on several factors, such as wasp-related factors, environmental factors, and ship-related factors.
Regulatory compliance
Though many regulations do not currently cover the extra safety and performance of WASP, the European Maritime Safety Agency (EMSA) recognizes its potential benefits in significantly reducing fuel consumption and emissions. The regulations have to be more unified to support the wider adoption of WASP.
Climate change mitigation
WASP technologies offer a promising way to reduce the maritime industry's carbon footprint. By lowering greenhouse gas emissions, following strict environmental rules, complying with other sustainable technologies, and offering economic advantages, WASP significantly contributes to worldwide efforts to decarbonize shipping and achieve sustainable maritime industry.
